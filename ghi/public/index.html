How does this work?

When users sign up for Halfway, they chose their MBTI to determine their personality type. MBTI helps us understand their preferences, characteristics, and compatibility with others. Based on this information, our algorithm suggests potential matches for each user.

The potential matches page on Halfway is where users can discover new people who align with their personality traits and have the potential for a meaningful connection. Every day, we update the potential matches list, ensuring that users have fresh options to explore.
